Why This Blog Exists

We all know things, but do we really know why they are the way they are?

In tech, we often accept facts as given — "VPC peering doesn't work with overlapping CIDRs", "Pods get their own IP", "Terraform needs a state file" — without ever asking why. These things are assumed to be common knowledge, yet when you dig deeper, you realize not everyone truly understands the reasoning behind them.

This blog is my attempt to answer the "why" behind the facts we take for granted. No jargon walls. No assumptions. Just clear explanations for curious minds who want to understand, not just memorize.
